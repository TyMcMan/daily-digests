Moebius: 0.2B Lightweight Image Inpainting Framework with 10B-Level Performance

Proposes the Local-λ Mix Interaction (LλMI) block — Local-λ and Interactive-λ modules — that compresses spatial contexts and semantic priors into fixed-size linear matrices, cutting a diffusion inpainting model to 0.22B parameters while matching 10B-scale quality. Paired with adaptive multi-granularity knowledge distillation, Moebius achieves over 15× faster inference than industrial-scale baselines.

Why it matters: Makes frontier-quality inpainting deployable on edge devices without a quality tradeoff — 0.22B parameters opens up on-device creative AI.

S-Agent: Spatial Tool-Use Elicits Reasoning for Spatial Intelligence

S-Agent is claimed to be the first agentic model purpose-built for spatial intelligence, decomposing 3D/4D tasks into perception-grounding steps then invoking specialized spatial tools at each reasoning step rather than relying on end-to-end inference. Substantially outperforms prior vision-language models on spatial benchmarks by treating spatial reasoning as a tool-augmented chain of thought.

Why it matters: Opens a new agentic paradigm for embodied and robotics tasks that need explicit spatial understanding — distinct from generic VLM capabilities.

Why it matters: A scalable, gradient-free path to lifelong robot skill accumulation using only language model inference — relevant to embodied AI and autonomous agent design.

Beyond Entropy: Learning from Token-Level Distributional Deviations for LLM Reasoning

Introduces Independent Combinatorial Tokens (ICT) for RLVR: rather than applying uniform entropy regularization to all tokens, ICT uses Jensen-Shannon divergence to identify "critical branching point" tokens and focuses optimization only there. Theoretical analysis using Shannon and Rényi entropy bounds proves ICT avoids both entropy collapse and entropy explosion during reasoning RL training.

Why it matters: Addresses a core instability in the dominant paradigm for training reasoning LLMs post-DeepSeek-R1 — a practical fix for teams doing RLVR post-training.
