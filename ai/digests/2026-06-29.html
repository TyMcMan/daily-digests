Native Active Perception as Reasoning for Omni-Modal Understanding

OmniAgent reframes long-video understanding as a POMDP-driven Observation–Thought–Action loop, selectively sampling audio-visual frames into persistent text memory rather than processing all frames at once. A 7B model trained this way outperforms Qwen2.5-VL-72B on video understanding benchmarks.
Why it matters: Demonstrates that intelligent frame selection beats throwing more parameters at the problem — a concrete blueprint for efficient, agent-native multimodal reasoning over long contexts. Accepted at ICML 2026.
