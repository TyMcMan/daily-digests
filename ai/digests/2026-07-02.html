DOPD: Dual On-Policy Distillation — Diagnosing and Fixing Privilege Illusion

Identifies "privilege illusion" as a core failure mode in on-policy distillation: the training signal conflates the teacher's genuine capability gap with information asymmetry the student can never replicate. DOPD dynamically routes token-level supervision between privileged teacher and privileged student policies based on advantage gap and relative probabilities.

Why it matters: On-policy distillation is increasingly central to training small reasoning models; this paper provides both a theoretical failure-mode diagnosis and a validated practical fix, with multi-institution corroboration.
