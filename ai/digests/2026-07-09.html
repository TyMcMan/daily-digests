The Flexibility Trap: Rethinking Arbitrary Order in Diffusion Language Models

ICML 2026 Outstanding Paper. Zanlin Ni et al. argue that arbitrary-order generation — a core selling point of diffusion language models — is actually a trap: models trained with arbitrary order underperform those trained with fixed order. They propose JustGRPO as a fix that recovers quality while preserving flexibility.
Why it matters: Challenges a foundational design choice in the rapidly growing DLM field; practical guidance for practitioners building on diffusion LM backbones.
