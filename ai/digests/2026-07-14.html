Self-Guided Test-Time Training for Long-Context LLMs

Proposes S-TTT, which selects only high signal-to-noise tokens from a long context for test-time adaptation, dramatically improving LLM performance on long-context tasks without expensive full-context fine-tuning. Authors: Xinyu Zhu, Zhe Xu, Xiaohan Wei et al.

Towards Mechanistically Understanding Why Memorized Knowledge Fails to Generalize in LLM Finetuning

Identifies a "knowledge–circuit misalignment" in LLM finetuning: newly memorized facts are stored in early layers but not routed to middle reasoning circuits, with a self-patching intervention recovering 58–75% of lost generalization. Authors: Lu Dai, Ziyang Rao, Yili Wang et al.

Why it matters: Explains why fine-tuned models answer direct recall prompts but fail at reasoning with the same facts — a core pain point for domain-specific SFT and the path to fixing it.
