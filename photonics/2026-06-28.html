Attenuation Scaling of 2f and 4f Free-Space Optical Matrix-Vector Multiply Architecturesnew

Comparative analysis of 2f vs. 4f free-space optical architectures for matrix-vector multiplication, finding that 4f designs exhibit more favorable attenuation scaling with matrix size — a key practical constraint for realizing large-dimension optical neural network layers in hardware. Why it matters: Clarifies which free-space ONN architecture class is more viable at practical matrix scales; actionable for anyone designing optical accelerator hardware.

Design Space Exploration of Hybrid III-V/Si Quantum Dot Microring Lasersnew

Systematic design space study for quantum dot microring lasers fabricated on hybrid III-V/silicon platforms, mapping the trade-offs between threshold current, output power, and cavity geometry for monolithic on-chip integration. Why it matters: On-chip laser sources remain a critical gap in silicon photonics; this design-space map accelerates wafer-level integration of laser sources without III-V bonding steps.
