emircbngl/blender-optics-simulator — AI/MCP-friendly Blender add-on for optical design and simulation

Blender add-on providing live beam tracing, kinematic-mount auto-alignment, polarization (Jones/Stokes), interference fringes, Gaussian beam propagation, wavelength-resolved optics, and a real-vendor component library. Exposes a headless optics_api designed to be driven by AI agents or MCP tools. Updated June 30, 2026. Why it matters: The MCP-friendly headless API makes this a practical substrate for agentic optical layout design — an emerging direction as groups explore AI-assisted free-space and fiber optical setup automation alongside PIC design.

WasatchPhotonics/Wasatch.NET — C# .NET driver for Wasatch Photonics spectrometers

Application-level C# .NET driver for Wasatch Photonics USB spectrometers, supporting wavelength calibration, dark correction, and intensity normalization. Updated June 30, 2026; long-maintained open-source instrument driver. Why it matters: Spectroscopy is a key photonic measurement tool; a well-maintained .NET driver lowers the integration barrier for labs and test benches running Windows-based photonic characterization stacks.
