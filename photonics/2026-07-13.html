PVA TePla and Fraunhofer IISB Establish Joint Laboratory for Aluminium Nitride Substratescatch-up

PVA TePla (wafer processing equipment) and Fraunhofer IISB (Erlangen, Germany) formed a joint laboratory targeting industrial AlN crystal growth and wafer production. AlN is an ultra-wide-bandgap semiconductor enabling UV photonics and high-power photonic pump laser sourcing on AlN-on-SiC and AlN-on-sapphire platforms — emerging alternatives beyond the capability ceiling of silicon photonics emitters. Why it matters: A European industrial-scale AlN substrate source reduces supply chain dependency on US and Asian vendors for next-generation pump lasers used in optical amplifier stacks relevant to optical compute interconnects.
