Vector Photonics Launches AMPPS PCSEL Packaging for AI Data Centers

Vector Photonics announced the AMPPS (Advanced Multi-Package Photonics Solution) platform, bringing Photonic Crystal Surface Emitting Lasers into ruggedised, integrable packages suitable for AI datacentre transceivers and optical network modules. PCSELs offer higher brightness and single-mode output than conventional VCSELs at equivalent drive current. Why it matters: a commercial PCSEL packaging solution narrows the gap between lab-demonstrated beam quality and volume production for next-generation optical links.

📄 Research & Labs

Ultrafast Programmable Bragg Reflection in Thin-Film Lithium Niobate Photonic Crystal (arXiv:2607.14565)

Researchers demonstrate sub-nanosecond reconfiguration of a Bragg-reflection stopband in a TFLN photonic crystal via electro-optic modulation, achieving greater than 30 dB extinction without mechanical tuning. The device integrates within a standard TFLN PIC fabrication flow, requiring no additional processing steps. Why it matters: ultrafast tunable reflectors are key building blocks for on-chip optical switching, optical memory, and photonic signal processing in integrated platforms.
